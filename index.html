What gets measured

Communication, not just grammar

Fluency & pace

Words per minute, pause frequency and rhythm across your answer.

Filler words

"Um", "uh" and "like" are counted and weighed against your total word count.

Vocabulary range

How varied your word choice is, not just how many words you used.

Pronunciation clarity

Estimated from how confidently the speech recognizer understood you.

On-camera presence

A lightweight read on eye contact, smiling and posture during your answer.

Upload a resume and get follow-ups built around your own experience.
